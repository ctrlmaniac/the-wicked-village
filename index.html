The Wicked Village

Your daughter has been kidnapped and all the clues brought you to a village.

You have to find your daughter at all costs!

It's so quiet that you almost fear that something is lurking, waiting for you...

How to play the game:
press F to go forward, R to go right,
B to go back, L to go left,
Y to say yes, N to say no,
H for help, I to check the inventory,
D to drop an item.
